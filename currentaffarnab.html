COVID-19 VACCINE PROGRESS,HOW FAR WE ARE

By Arnab Roy

Currently the most debatable topic in the world is that When will humanity get its first Corona virus vaccine.Its currently over 9 months that the virus have infected the human population of the World,and undoubtedly after the Spanish Flu this pandemic is the biggest viral threat to the human population of the World.

Many countries are doing extensive research for the development of the Corona Virus vaccine namely Britain,USA,Russia,China,India,Israel and many others.Currently more than 160 companies are working on the development and trial of the Covid-19 Vaccine.

There are many phases of the vaccine development Including-:

1}Development of the Vaccine
2}Pre-Clinical trial
3}Clinical Trials
4}Regulatory Approval
5}Manufacturing
History says Development of Vaccine is a long process and takes lot of time and research,Previously amny vaccines were discovered but those took many years for its development.

Clinical trials is the most important phase where vaccine is administerd in Human body and the effects are studied.

Let us have a look at the where are different countries standing in vaccine development.

Britain
According to the latest reports the vaccine developed by the Oxford University is in third phase of clinical trials,The first two trials have given the expected results. UNITED STATES America have also moved ahead with the vaccine development with an American company named Moderna is currently developing the vaccine.They are in the last phase of clinical trials,and they will start the trial from 27th July in defferent locations in America.

INDIA
Two companies are currently in the advanced level of vaccine development in India.The first one is Bharat Biotech,A hyderbad based company and another company named Zydus.Bharat Biotech is working on the vaccine named Covaxin and Zydus is working on ZyCov-D,both these vaccines are in the first and second phase of clinical trials respectively.

RUSSIA
The researchers of a Russian University have claimd to launch the Covid vaccine as early as August.The Moscow State Medical University have claimed that they have successfully completed all the clinical trials with the desired effect.

CHINA
A Chineese company named Sinovac Biotech have claimed to complete the two clinical trials and are now in the third or last phase of clinical trials .Currently 4 different companies are developing the Covid-19 vaccine in China. GERMANY German companies have claimed to reach the second phase of clinical trials,and the same is true for a Australian company.

MISSION CHANDRAYAN-2,THE SUCCESS STORY THE WORLD MISSED.

By Arnab Roy

On 22nd July 2019 ISRO launced its most ambitious project THE CHANDRAYAN-2 from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre with the most famous and powerfull rocket GSLV MARK-III M1,its been 1 year since then and the world recognizes it as a failed mission.The Vikram lander was scheduled to land on 7th September 2019 after 47 days,but it failed to establish a soft landing and had hard landing instead.

They say failure is the part of the game and it shows the way towards success but the condition is you must not give up.The World have seen the photos of how the ISRO director broken down in tears in the shoulders of the Prime Minister when the Vikram lander failed to land successfully in the South Polar Region of the moon.This was the first time any lander tried to land in the South

When ISRO lost the initial contact with Vikram lander just 2 kilometeres away from the moon surface whole country became emotional.135 crore people of India was alive that day with the Prime Minister and the ISRO officials to witness this historical event.Foreign media have just put a statement that Chandrayan 2 mission is failed but in this article we are going to put forward for you some analysis that would prove that this mission is not a failed mission,maybe it is not successful to the extent we thought but it has done the initial job pretty well.

When Vikram Lander crash landed ISRO had given a statement that CHANDRAYAN-2 mission is 95% successful,as the landing may not have been as planned but the orbiter that was orbiting the moon is doing its job successfully,and all the 8 payloads of the orbiter is working correctly and providing high resolution pictures an data of the Moon surface.ISRO also published the 3 D images of the Lindberg Crater taken by the terrain mapping camera of the orbiter.

Now,After the completion of 1 year of the Chandrayan-2, ISRO have stated that the orbiter have finished covering 60% of the Moon terrain,and with the help of this data ISRO will be able to exactly tellthat how much water is present in the moon surface.most importantly the instruments present in the orbiter are providing data of the moon surface upto 4 kilometers depth which is enabling the scientists of ISRO to do the mineral mapping inside the moon surface.ISRO have told that the scientific date received from the orbiter would be published in October 2020 for the World and this obiter would continue doing its job formany more years to come.

With the most cheapest budget ISRO have done what some of the big space agencies around the World with huge budgets have failed to do.ISRO is also planning to do Chandrayan 3 mission and also planning a manned space mission as early as 2022.
